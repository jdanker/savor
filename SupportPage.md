# Savor — Support

Savor is a personal restaurant tracking app for iOS. Save places you want to try, rate ones you've visited, and browse your list whenever you need inspiration.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I add a restaurant?**
Tap the **+** button and search for a restaurant by name. Select it from the suggestions to add it to your list.

**Can I edit or delete a restaurant I've saved?**
Yes — swipe left on any restaurant in your list to delete it.

**My restaurant search isn't returning results. What should I try?**
Check that your device has an active internet connection. Restaurant search requires network access to query Google Places.

**Does Savor back up my data?**
Savor stores your list locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled in iOS Settings, your app data is included in those device backups.

**Does Savor share my data with anyone?**
No. Your saved restaurants, ratings, and notes never leave your device. See the full [Privacy Policy](/savor/privacy) for details.
